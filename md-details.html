Adari Ramakrishna serves as the Managing Director of ARK Infra. He brings extensive expertise in high-level land procurement, legal joint venture contracting, and corporate asset planning. His primary focus is on identifying high-growth developmental corridors and formulating long-term strategies that benefit both land partners and homeowners.

With an established track record in land development negotiations, Mr. Ramakrishna is responsible for the legal compliance, verification process, and site acquisition stages for all ongoing developments. His work ensures that every layout launched under the ARK Infra brand stands on legally clear titles, preventing delays and securing clean spot registrations for customers.

Collaborating directly with municipal planning boards, surveyors, and financial institutions, he leads the efforts that secure bank loan eligibilities and VMRDA layout approvals. His strict oversight creates a solid framework of safety and commercial viability for all residential plot subdivisions and luxury housing investments.
